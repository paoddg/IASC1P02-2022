school

I am a fourth year Dramatic Arts student at Brock University, and I'm set to graduate in June 2020! I mainly focus in production courses because I'm hoping to work as a stage manager after graduation. I've already stage managed 2 full productions and 1 staged reading here at the school. You can see more of my work on my resume page (I'm available for hire starting May 2020!). You might have realized by now that I am by no means a web developer. I got interested in this course because my boyfriend is a programmer and he kept saying that I'd find this kind of stuff fun, so I decided to make IASC 1P02 one of my electives in my final year. I think it was one of the best decisions I've ever made, because although it's really challenging, I'm slowly but surely learning some really cool stuff.

interests

In the last couple of years I've been getting more into playing video games. I mainly play PC games but sometimes I'll change it up with some PS4, 3DS, and even some mobile games if I'm laying in bed looking for something to pass the time. I also really like dogs. A lot of my time at home is spent taking care of my dog, going on walks with her, or taking her to the dog park. After she's all tired out, she sits by me on the couch while I play video games. I went to the gym and did some weight lifting for the first time in my life this week, and although I haven't yet made it part of my routine, I'd love to one day be able to call working out an interest of mine.

goals

My main goal after graduation is to be able to support myself through just stage management, which is a pretty lofty thing in itself. I'd also love to have a nice house with a big backyard and huge windows so I can always be close to the outdoors (but still be able to stay in when it's too cold outside). I'd also love to bring a couple more dogs into the mix. The thing about theatre is that it's not really a trusty way to pay the bills, so one of my other goals is to stick with programming and keep it fun for myself (and maybe I'll be able to make some money off it on the side). Another one of my goals is to become more fit! I think that a person's mental health and physical health are very much tied in with each other, and so I've recently been trying to work both of those further up my list of priorities, so I can make taking care of myself a habit before my body starts to turn on me. So I guess one of my goals is just to be a happier and healthier person all around.
